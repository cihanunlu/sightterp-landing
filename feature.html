Sight‑Terp
Assist

Your digital boothmate that offers cloud-based speech recognition tailored for interpreters. It provides real-time transcription in more than 20 languages with minimal delay, giving professionals reliable on-the-spot support during demanding meetings. The tool automatically highlights important units in the speech, in segmented format.


Low‑latency ASR you can trust: fast, stable speech recognition tuned for live delivery
Selective translation: source‑only, target‑only, or both—switch instantly without leaving your flow
Entity highlighting: automatic emphasis on numbers, names, dates, and terminology
Glossary‑aware context: import a CSV/XLSX and boost recognition where it matters
Speaker diarization (optional): follow who said what at a glance
Notes and exports: capture transcripts, copy key segments, and export after the session
Privacy by design: encrypted transport; no sales of your data

20+ languages for ASR/translation, including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Turkish, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Dutch, Danish, Norwegian, Swedish, Polish, Russian, Portuguese, Finnish, Hindi.


1) Pick source and target languages
2) Select mic and start recognition
3) See live transcript; click or select text to translate on the fly
4) Search or pin terms; glossary suggestions surface automatically
5) Enable speaker diarization to follow who said what
5) Export transcripts in multiple formats


Free starter: begin with complimentary credits to try live ASR, translation, and highlighting

Sight‑Terp
Evaluate

Sight-Terp Evaluate transforms your practice recordings into actionable insights. Upload audio and receive automatic, rubric-based feedback with clear strengths, areas for improvement, and visual dashboards that track your progress.


Accuracy: Word-for-word comparison between source and target
Fluency: Speech rate, pauses, and natural flow assessment
Completeness: Coverage of source content and information retention
Terminology: Technical term usage and consistency
Disfluencies: Repetitions, false starts, and self-corrections
Cohesion: Logical flow and coherence of interpretation


1) Upload your source audio and interpretation recording
2) AI automatically transcribes both audio files
3) System compares source and target using advanced algorithms
4) Generate comprehensive feedback report with scores
5) View visual dashboards showing progress over time
6) Export detailed reports in DOCX format


Visual Dashboard: Interactive charts showing performance trends
Detailed Report: Comprehensive DOCX document with specific feedback
Score Breakdown: Individual scores for each evaluation criterion
Recommendations: Specific areas for improvement and practice


In-memory processing: All analysis happens in memory, no permanent storage
No data retention: Your recordings are not saved on our servers
Encrypted uploads: Secure transmission of your audio files
Educational focus: Designed for learning and skill development
GDPR compliant: Full compliance with data protection regulations
