Refund Policy — Sight‑Terp Toolbox

Effective date: 22.10.2025

1) General Refund Policy

We offer refunds for Sight‑Terp services according to the following schedule:

  • Within 14 days of purchase: Full refund available
  • After 14 days: No refunds available

2) Digital Services and Credits

  • Credits purchased for Sight‑Terp services (Assist minutes, Evaluate runs, etc.) follow the same refund schedule as other purchases.
  • Credits are consumed upon use and cannot be refunded or transferred once used.
  • Unused credits are eligible for refunds according to the time-based schedule above.

3) Subscription Services

  • Monthly and annual subscriptions follow the same refund schedule as other purchases.
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future charges.
  • Refunds for subscriptions are calculated based on the time-based schedule above.

4) Free Trials

  • Free trials are provided at no cost and are not eligible for refunds.
  • If you are charged during a free trial period due to automatic billing, you are eligible for refund based on the terms of the subscription.

5) Technical Issues and Service Availability

  • We strive to maintain high service availability, but we do not guarantee uninterrupted service.
  • Service interruptions or technical issues may qualify for refunds if they occur within the refund period.
  • We may provide service credits or extensions at our discretion for significant service disruptions.

6) Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

  • If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute, your account may be suspended pending resolution.
  • Fraudulent chargebacks may result in permanent account termination.
  • We reserve the right to dispute unwarranted chargebacks.

7) Legal Rights and Consumer Protection

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under applicable consumer protection laws. If you are entitled to a refund under local law, we will honor such requirements.

8) Refund Processing

  • If a refund is approved, it will be processed within 5‑10 business days.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

9) Contact for Refund Requests

If you believe you are entitled to a refund under this policy or applicable law, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@sightterp.com
  • Include your account details and reason for the refund request
  • We will review your request and respond within 5 business days

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted with a new effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11) Merchant of Record

Sight‑Terp uses a Merchant of Record (MoR) service for payment processing. All transactions are subject to the MoR's terms and conditions in addition to our refund policy. The MoR handles payment processing, tax collection, and compliance with local regulations.
